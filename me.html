Hi, I'm Alexander Niklass, a 23-years-old Communication Design Student based in Berlin. I was born in Ulaan Bataar and moved to Germany when I was 3 years old. Since my childhood I have done creative stuff of all kind. I was raised in a small northern German town and after an apprenticeship I decided to make my hobby becoming professional. So I enroled myself in the HTW Berlin in Oktober 2015. Since then I started to take pictures of moody moments in Berlin. I like to create a feeling of loneliness and anonymity in the photos which are taking place mostly at night. I hope you enjoy them and stay tuned for some awesome illustrations in near future!
